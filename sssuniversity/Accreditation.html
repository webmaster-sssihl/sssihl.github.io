NAAC
The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) is an organisation that assesses and accredits higher education Institutions (HEIs) in India. It is an autonomous body funded by University Grants Commission, Government of India.
The documents listed below are mandated to be published on the website by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) for the purpose of the assessment process towards accreditation.
They include the Quantitative Metrics (QnM) – which are part of the Self Study Report – and a full list of syllabuses for all programmes offered at SSSIHL.

Quantitative Metrics (QnM)
  • Structured feedback received from Students (1.4.1)
  • The Institute’s Policy document on Promotion of Research (3.1.1)
  • Policy document on Consultancy (3.5.1)
  • Handbook on Code of Conduct for students and teachers, manuals and brochures on human values and professional ethics (7.1.12)
  • The Institute’s functioning as per professional code of prescribed / suggested by statutory bodies / regulatory authorities for different professions (7.1.12)
  • Best Practices: Social Relevant Research & Teaching Quality Feedback (7.2.1)

NAD
The National Academic Depository (NAD) is an online store house of all academic awards (certificates, diplomas, degrees, marksheets, etc.) duly digitised and lodged by academic institutions / boards / eligibility assessment bodies. NAD not only ensures easy access to and retrieval of an academic award but also validates and guarantees its authenticity and safe storage. For more information, visit the NAD website

AQAR
Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) is a useful document that gives the overall picture of the university’s growth in all the seven criteria identified by NAAC. It also provides systematic data with respect to various improvements taken up by the University. It is submitted to NAAC every year as per the format prescribed.

NCTE
The National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) is a statutory body of the Indian government that formally oversees standards, procedures and processes in the Indian education system.
