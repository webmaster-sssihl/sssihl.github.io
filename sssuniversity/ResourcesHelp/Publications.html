Summer Course in Indian Culture & Spirituality
The Summer Course in Indian Culture & Spirituality serves as an induction course to all students of the University. The three day event takes place during the second week of each Academic Year. The books chronologically represent the entire programme of the Summer Course in Indian Culture and Spirituality. This includes all the speeches, interactive sessions such as the Parayanam sessions (experiences with Bhagawan Baba), panel discussions and presentations.. The themes of the talk are related to Indian Culture and Spirituality, and will give the reader a glimpse of the vast and ancient wisdom of Bharat.

Prasanthi Vidwan Mahasabha
These books are a compilation of all the talks delivered during the Prasanthi Vidwan Mahasabha - a series of evening sessions of talks delivered at Sai Kulwant Hall, Prasanthi Nilayam during the Dasara festival each year. The 'Vidwan Mahasabha’ consists of speakers — primarily teachers and students of SSSIHL and functionaries of Bhagawan Baba’s Institutions — addressing the gathering on topics concerning spirituality and philosophy, including the teachings of Sri Sathya Sai Baba and experiences of devotees. Relevant transcripts of excerpts from Bhagawan Baba’s Divine discourses are also included.
